# How to use Flâneur (this build)

You need to be in a **live multiplayer-style session** so the app can talk to a shared session store. 
Flâneur is meant for **small groups**, ideally **two to four people** in the same session.

---

## What you’re doing in the experience

You’re walking around together. You can **see where your friends are** (compass-style cues), **drop pins** on things you notice, and **steer toward a friend’s pin** without texting “where are you?” The pin list and a **Navigation** line tell the group who’s heading where.

---

## Dropping a pin

1. **Close the sidebar** (the pin list) before you try—while it’s open, the app **on purpose** won’t drop a pin, so you don’t accidentally place one when you meant to tap a button.
2. **Look at something in the real world**—a wall, a stall, the floor, whatever has mesh the glasses can see.
3. Use the **hand ray** (Spectacles’ usual “point with your hand” interaction). When you **let go of the trigger** on a good hit, that’s when the pin is placed. You might see a small **preview** while you’re aiming.

If nothing appears: make sure you’re in a **real session** (see above), sidebar is **closed**, and you’re actually hitting **world geometry** (not empty air).

---

## The pin list and “take me there”

Open the **sidebar** to see pins from everyone in the session.

- **Tap a row** for a pin you care about. That tells your compass to **point at that pin** (like “guide me here”). Labels read in a natural way, e.g. **“Jamie’s pin.”**
- **Navigate** — join whatever pin target the group last had in play (match the shared “we’re going here” vibe).
- **Reset** — stop following a pin and go back to **following people** instead.

There’s also a **Navigation** line in the UI that stays put: it’s the steady place to read **who’s navigating where**, not the little pop-up toasts (those are just quick “something happened” pings).

---

## What isn’t in this prototype

These are **not** implemented in the version you’re demoing:

- **Meet here** beacons, **ghost pins** when you come back another day, **end-of-session recap collages**, or **saving to the cloud**.
- **Points, challenges,** or a **reaction sticker shop** — those are product vision, not this build.
- **Tapping the 3D pin floating in the room** to navigate — the reliable path is **tap the pin in the sidebar list**.
- Pins that **stick around for weeks** or fade on a timer the way the long-term spec describes — this milestone is about the **live session** together.
